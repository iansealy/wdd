Task

Continue working on your mock-up and try some of the following:

  • Use the vector shape tool to draw 4 or 5 areas of colour on your mockup.
  • Pick a colour scheme of 4 or 5 colours. You can use Kuler or Colour scheme designer to help if you wish.
  • Use the coloured shapes to pick out areas of the page. Think about using coloured shapes to indicate areas of the page for navigation or a footer.

OR

Start on a new layout for your module 3 assignment.
