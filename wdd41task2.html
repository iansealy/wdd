Task

  • Read the tutorials
  • Create a feedback form for your own Web site
  • Your form should collect the user's name, email address, age and comments
  • Download Tectite FormMail and integrate it with your contact form
  • (In brief, you'll need to download the script, unzip it, open it in Brackets and make appropriate changes to the script (editing $TARGET_EMAIL at a minimum), upload it to your Web space and finally change your form in a similar way to sampleform.htm.)
