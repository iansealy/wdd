Task

  • Read the tutorials
  • Create a feedback form for your own Web site
  • Your form should collect the user's name, email address, age and comments
  • Install Jem’s Free PHP Mail Form and integrate it with your contact form
  • (In brief, you'll need to download the script, unzip it, open it in Brackets and make appropriate changes at the top of the script, change the file extension to .php, upload it to your Web space and finally change the form's action to point at the script.)
