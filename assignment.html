Assignments

The Postgraduate Certificate in Web Design and Development is based around the completion of four module assignments:

  • WDD1 Assignment
  • WDD2 Assignment
  • WDD3 Assignment
  • WDD4 Assignment

Submission

All students are required to submit two copies of their assignments - a hard copy and an electronic copy. The hard copy will be returned to you six weeks after publication of your final results. The electronic copy should be submitted via Blackboard.

To upload an electronic copy of your assignment, go to Blackboard and login. Select the relevant module and then select Assignments from the left-hand navigation bar. To submit click on View/Complete.

You can submit the hard copy by post using recorded delivery or in person. If you prefer to do the latter, please leave it in the drop box in the reception of 43 Gordon Square between 8am and 8pm, Monday to Friday.

Please remember that the hard copy of your assignment must be accompanied by a filled in and signed coursework coversheet. These will be distributed by tutors but you can also collect them from reception at 43 Gordon Square.

Deadlines

The key dates you must remember are:

Module Deadline
WDD1 Tuesday, 13th December 2011
WDD2 Tuesday, 28th February 2012
WDD3 Tuesday, 22nd May 2012
WDD4 Tuesday, 24th July 2012

No individual member of staff can grant extensions without penalty beyond the published final submission deadline, so please do not ask them to do so.

Any work that is submitted for formal assessment after the Final submission deadline for coursework portfolios will be subject to a Penalty mark, unless mitigating circumstances are accepted by the Mitigation Sub-committee of the Board of Examiners. A subsequent deadline follows this penalty period, after which work will not be marked and will be returned to the student.

Please refer to the Student Handbook for submission deadlines and Mitigating Circumstances procedure.
