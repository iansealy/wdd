WDD3

WDD3 aims to develop skills to evaluate Web sites, analyse and document the needs of stakeholders and manage and design information for the Web. The module covers information architecture, optimal navigation techniques, evaluation techniques (user analysis, task analysis, stakeholder analysis), project management and project costing.
