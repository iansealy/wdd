Home

Welcome to the Web site for the Birkbeck Postgraduate Certificate in Web Design and Development.

The site is intended to support you during the course. It contains details about the staff teaching the course, as well as some information about all the students. More importantly, the modules section contains full notes for each session. There is also information about the mailing lists used by us to contact you and by you to contact us and each other. Finally, there are details of each of the four assignments, a timetable and a reading list.
