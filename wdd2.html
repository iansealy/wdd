WDD2

WDD2 aims to develop skills to evaluate Web sites, analyse and document the needs of stakeholders, and manage and design information for the Web. The course covers the importance of a user-centred approach to planning and designing websites, covering research, design and evaluation methods. This emphasis allows the students to create websites that will be more valuable to users and businesses alike. The module covers information architecture, optimal navigation techniques, evaluation techniques, (user analysis, task analysis, stakeholder analysis), project management and project costing.
