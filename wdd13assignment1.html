WDD1 Assignment

WDD1 assignment is due on Tuesday, 15th December 2009.

Your assignments will ideally all be related. That is, the assignments for WDD1, WDD2 and WDD3 should build towards your final project (WDD4 assignment). This is not essential (your circumstances might change), but is preferable.

You should discuss your choice of topic with me at some point today or during the next session, so that I can ensure that it's feasible and covers the range of skills that you'll be acquiring during the course.

Note: If you reverse engineer a site to create the user interface for your home page for WDD1 assignment then make sure that I can't recognise the site that you reverse engineered!
